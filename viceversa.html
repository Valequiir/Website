BACKGROUND

This is a project is made for a contest where the objective is to develop an idea that can contribute minimally with one of the 17 sustainable development goals (SDG’s) that are part of the 2030 agenda adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015. The context that I decided to follow to realize the concept was based on knowledge familiar from my home country of Colombia and some of its current problems, because for me it is essential to take advantage of all the opportunities and contribute to the progress of my country.

PROBLEM

The increase of the Colombian diaspora around the world, generating social problem due to the flight of talent, leaving the country in disadvantage in the intellectual, business and economic.

HOPE

Maintain close relationship between Colombia and its diaspora. Promoting an exchange of benefits at an intellectual, economic, human level to obtain recogntion and opportunities on the part of the Colombian state.

SOLUTION

The only digital platform that connects Colombia with their citizens living abroad to share the current knowledge of other countries and contribute to growth and social development.
